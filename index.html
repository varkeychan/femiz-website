About Femiz

Where every detail matters

Femiz is a premium home-based nail studio curated by Snehha — built for girls who believe beautiful nails are part of their identity, not just an accessory. Every set is thoughtfully designed with precision, softness, and attention to detail to create a luxury experience that feels personal, feminine, and modern.

Quiet luxury, made personal. “Beautiful nails aren’t just an accessory — they’re part of how you express yourself.” - Snehha (Founder, Artist)
